Diagnostic AI Systems

AI Ophthalmic Models

Three validated deep learning models for automated retinal disease detection, designed for integration into clinical and community screening workflows.

MODEL 01
👁️
AMD

Age-Related Macular Degeneration

Detects and grades AMD severity from fundus photographs. Identifies drusen, pigmentary changes, geographic atrophy, and choroidal neovascularisation.

Level 0 — No Macular Degeneration
Level 1 — Mild Macular Degeneration

MODEL 02
🩸
DR

Diabetic Retinopathy

Five-level ETDRS-aligned severity grading. Detects microaneurysms, haemorrhages, exudates, venous beading, and neovascularisation at disc and elsewhere.

Level 0 — No DR
Level 1 — Mild
Level 2 — Moderate
Level 3 — Severe
Level 4 — Proliferative DR

MODEL 03
🔵
CDR

Glaucoma — CDR Estimation

Automated cup-to-disc ratio measurement from disc-centred fundus images. Provides continuous CDR estimate with clinical threshold interpretation.

CDR 0.3–0.5 — Medium / normal
CDR 0.5–0.6 — Large, context-dependent
CDR >0.6 — Suspicious, refer
