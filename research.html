Stroke Detection: Our Primary Focus

We have a particular focus on stroke detection through retinal imaging. The retina provides a unique window into the cardiovascular system, allowing us to detect signs of stroke risk before clinical symptoms appear.

Research Impact

Our research aims to bridge the gap between routine eye care and comprehensive health screening. By leveraging the retina as a window to systemic health, we can detect life-threatening conditions like stroke before they manifest clinically, potentially saving thousands of lives annually.

Through our partnerships with leading clinical institutions and access to extensive datasets, we are advancing the frontier of AI-powered preventive medicine.
