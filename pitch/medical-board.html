Dr. Levai Lehar
Ophthalmology specialist - Vitreum Clinics in Satu Mare, Oradea, Zalău, Beiuș, and Vitreum Oradea Rogerius.
Clinical Profile
Professional summary

Dr. Levai Lehar has medical and surgical expertise across vitreoretinal surgery, cataract surgery, refractive surgery (including PRESBYOND and ReLEx SMILE), corneal transplant, FemtoLASIK procedures, and glaucoma surgery.

His practice focuses on complex vision-threatening conditions such as retinal detachments, vitreous hemorrhages, and advanced anterior and posterior segment pathology.

He is also involved in advanced ophthalmic techniques, including ocular endoscopy, for selected intraocular evaluation and treatment cases.

Dr. Liviu Bran
Senior ophthalmology consultant - Polisano Clinical Hospital Sibiu.
Clinical Institution
Professional summary

Dr. Liviu Bran is Coordinator of the Ophthalmology Department at the European Polisano Hospital in Sibiu, with clinical focus on comprehensive ophthalmic care and surgical management.

His training includes medical studies at the Iuliu Hațieganu University of Medicine and Pharmacy, residency in ophthalmology in Cluj-Napoca, and international clinical exposure in France and the United Kingdom.

He completed advanced courses in cataract, retinal detachment, refractive, glaucoma, and vitreoretinal surgery, including specialized programs in London, Geneva, Copenhagen, Vancouver, Sofia, and Thessaloniki.
